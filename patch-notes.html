Patch Notes: 2/9/2021

- Fixed Achievement Laurels, they should now properly work along with achievement points

- Fixed the rest of the Skill Advancements, they should now properly show the halved price- and cost accordingly

- Fixed the rest of the Basic skills, they should now properly have all their costs divided by 10

- Fixed the drop rate of Wooden-, Silver- and Golden boxes; they will now properly drop only one of either in dungeons

- Increased the amount of VIP exp for vanguards heavily, (PvE and PvP)

- Implemented credits crates for several vanguards, (PvE and PvP)

- Adjusted the hp- and defense of guild BAM's

- Halved the cost of repairing gear

- Put card boxes in Elleon's Mark's shop

- Put the Master's Enchantment Box in the Elleon's Mark's shop

- Increased the drop rate of Dark Shard boxes for larger monster significantly

- Made a temporary fix for Crystalbinds, we've put complete crystalbinds in the Vanguard shop at 1 credit each, and they're stackable - protect your crystals, friends!

- Vanguards should now be resetting in the morning, rather than in the afternoon

- Ghillieglade scrolls can now be bought from the Vanguard shop for non Tera Club members

- Added 1.5 skill to Tera Club, (Ruler of Dragons), along with 1 strongbox key a day from the Supply Box, (30 keys in 30 days)

- Added 2.0 Dragon to Dragonscale shop

- Release of webshop + homepage coming super soon now!
